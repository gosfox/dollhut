> But I don't have GitHub!!!! >:(

1. Click "Continue with GitHub"

You'll be taken to the GitHub sign-up page. Pick whichever option is easiest for you (Continue with Google/Apple will fill in your email and password automatically):

2. Information

Enter a username and your region. If you're not comfortable sharing your location, put whatever — Dollhut never sees it, GitHub just asks. If you're asked for a code, check the email you signed up with and paste it in.

3. Log in

After all that, log in with what you just set up, authorize Dollhut, and you'll land on your dashboard.
